# NFPA 13 Quick Reference

Common values used in commands. Always verify against the current edition of NFPA 13.

## Sprinkler Spacing (Light Hazard)
- Maximum coverage per sprinkler: 225 sq ft
- Maximum spacing: 15 ft
- Minimum spacing: 6 ft (to avoid cold soldering)

## Sprinkler Spacing (Ordinary Hazard)
- Maximum coverage per sprinkler: 130 sq ft
- Maximum spacing: 15 ft

## Deflector-to-Ceiling Distance
- Pendent/Upright (unobstructed): 1" to 12" below ceiling
- Sidewall: per listing

## Minimum Pipe Sizes
- 1" for branch lines serving 1 sprinkler
- 1-1/4" for branch lines serving 2 sprinklers
- Refer to pipe schedule or hydraulic calculations for larger layouts

## Hanger Spacing
- Maximum: 12 ft for 1" through 1-1/4" pipe
- Maximum: 12 ft for 1-1/2" and larger pipe
- Within 12" of each coupling/fitting on pipes 1" and smaller

## Notes
- This is a quick reference only — not a substitute for the full standard
- Values may vary by occupancy classification and local amendments
